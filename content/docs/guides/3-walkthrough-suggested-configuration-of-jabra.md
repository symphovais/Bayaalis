#### 3 Walkthrough: Suggested configuration of Jabra+

### For Jabra personal devices :

- Pair your Bluetooth Jabra personal devices to the USB dongle (Jabra Link Bluetooth adapter)
- Create device group(s) and sort Jabra personal devices

### For Jabra meeting room devices :

- Add a locations structure based on how the business organization is organized.
- o Move your unassigned rooms into locations and add details to rooms
- Defining a schedule for Jabra meeting room device updates

### Other optional tasks to complete for both personal and meeting room devices :

- Configurations: use standard configuration with or without exceptions (For Personal or Meeting room devices) or create one i.e.: Create a configuration/Create a room configuration
- o Assigning a configuration or Bulk assigning a previously created room configuration to many rooms
- - 
- Firmware: You can customize firmware policies in configurations to suit your needs
- o Enable or disable firmware management
- o Choose whether you want to Modify &amp; set the firmware policy for specific Jabra device models
- o Choose a specific firmware version for your Jabra devices to use, and others.

For reference information and to understand all the capabilities of the platform, see the Jabra+ platform management chapter.
