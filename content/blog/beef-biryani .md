### **Instructions**  

#### **Step 1: Marinate the Beef**  
1. In a large bowl, combine:  
   - 1.5 kg beef (with bones)  
   - 340 gm curd (plain yogurt)  
   - 150 gm crushed fried onions  
   - 115 gm ginger garlic paste  
   - 4 tbsp fried onion oil  
   - 4 green chilies (mild, slit)  
   - 1-2 green chilies (spicy, slit)  
   - 23 gm Degi red chili powder  
   - 23 gm coriander powder  
   - 5 gm caraway seeds (Shahi Jeera)  
   - 15 gm fresh mint leaves (chopped)  
   - 2 tbsp coriander stems (chopped)  
   - 35 gm salt  
2. Mix well to coat the beef evenly. Cover and marinate for at least **2 hours** (overnight is even better).  

---

#### **Step 2: Prepare Biryani Masala**  
1. Dry roast the following on low heat:  
   - 15 gm coriander seeds, 8 gm cumin seeds, 23 gm black peppercorns, 3 gm mace, 3 gm green cardamom, 2 gm black cardamom, 2 gm cloves, 1.5 gm cinnamon stick, and 6-7 bay leaves.  
2. Grind them into a fine powder. Use **2.5 tsp** of this masala later.

#### **Step 6: Layer the Biryani**  
1. In a heavy-bottom pot, add half of the cooked beef as the first layer.
