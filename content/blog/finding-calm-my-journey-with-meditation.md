In a last-ditch effort for some peace, I downloaded the Headspace app. I'd always been skeptical about meditation, but I was desperate. To my surprise, those ten minutes of guided stillness were a revelation. The drive home was still noisy, but it felt like the volume had been dialed down. Since then, meditation has become a tool I use whenever I'm feeling overwhelmed or need to calm my nerves before a big presentation.
